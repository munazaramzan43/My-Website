Dr. Munaza Ramzan, Assistant Professor PGDM & MIB, IMS Noida

I am M-Tech in Computer Science & Engineering & pursuing Ph.D. During my research & teaching career of more than 4 years, I have taught subjects like Web Technologies, asp.net, Data structures and Python. I participated in some of the International conferences of repute such as IEEE, and others. I have published a number of research papers in International and national journals and conferences. I have presented a Poster entitled with “Analyzing emotional brain functional connectivity networks using Electroencephalography signals” in Australasian Winter Conference on Brain Research (AWCBR) New-Zealand. I am the active reviewer in Biocybernetics and Biomedical Engineering Journal.
